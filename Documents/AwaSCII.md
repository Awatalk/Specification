
# AwaSCII

The **A**wa **S**tandard **C**ode for **I**nformation **I**nterchange  
is a concentrated form of the widely used **ASCII**.

<br>

## Common AwaSCII

This redefined form of **AwaSCII** has been  
designed for better compatibility with **ASCII**.
